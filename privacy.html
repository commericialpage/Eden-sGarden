Privacy Policy

At Eden's Garden, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, contact details, delivery address, and order details provided during checkout.
  • Communication Information: messages or inquiries you send us through our website or Telegram.
  • Technical Information: browser type, device type, and IP address (collected automatically through standard web logs).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and confirm your flower orders.
  • Arrange delivery and provide customer support.
  • Communicate with you about your order via Telegram or email.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Payments via Telegram

Payments are completed through Telegram communication with our merchant. While we make every effort to ensure safe transactions, we are not responsible for Telegram’s handling of your information. Please review Telegram’s own Privacy Policy for more details.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and website performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not work correctly without them.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share your information only when necessary:

  • With delivery partners to fulfill your order.
  • To comply with legal obligations if required by law.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to our legal and operational obligations.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
